Proposal for Collaboration: Learning Utsav 2024

Dear {{name}},

I hope this email finds you well! We are writing to you on behalf of Learning Utsav, and we are excited to invite {{name}} to join us as a Sponsor for Learning Utsav, a 30-day skill-building challenge. Learning Utsav is a KEC Electronics Club initiative made possible with support from the KEC Computer Club. This event will take place during the Dashain-Tihar break and aims to help students and professionals across Nepal enhance their skills in fields like electronics, software/web development, AI/data science, cybersecurity, and cloud technologies.

Our Vision

Learning Utsav is a campaign aimed at motivating people to upskill during the festive break. We envision it as a festival of learning that communities can take part in every year, turning the downtime of Dashain-Tihar into a productive learning experience. We aim to make this an annual tradition where students and professionals across Nepal can engage, skill up, and grow during their holidays.

How Learning Utsav Works:

  • Duration: From Ghatasthapana to Bhaitika (33 days total, minimum 30 posts required)
  • Goal: Participants will commit to daily learning and post on social media about what they’ve learned.
  • Categories: Learners can focus on areas such as electronics, software/web development, AI/data science, cybersecurity, or cloud technologies.
  • Daily Posts: Participants must post daily, sharing their progress. They need to post a minimum of 30 days to qualify for rewards, with a maximum of 33 days.
  • Rewards for Consistency: The most consistent learners will be rewarded at the end of the challenge.

Your Role as a Collaborator:

As a collaborator, {name} will play a key role in promoting a culture of learning during this festive season. Your responsibilities include:

  • Moderator Assignment: One representative from your community will be assigned as a Moderator to guide learners and help manage the campaign. The Moderator must be active; if they are not contributing effectively, we may request a replacement.
  • Student Participation & Monitoring: The Moderator will have access to your community’s learning progress to monitor student participation and ensure learners remain consistent in their daily posts.
  • Problem-Solving Support: Help students with any challenges they face during the learning period, ensuring they stay on track.
  • Identifying Top Learners: Recommend the most consistent learners for rewards.
  • Support for Associated Programs: If you organize any program aligned and branded with Learning Utsav, we’ll help promote it and highlight your efforts.

What We Provide:

  • Providing Rewards: Consistent learners will be rewarded.
  • Branding Support: We will provide logos, banners, and other promotional materials for the campaign.
  • Guidelines: Simple guidelines and resources will be shared to help you plan and manage the event within your community.
  • Event Promotion: Your event and contributions will be promoted through our website and social media channels for maximum visibility.

Next Steps:

If you’re interested in partnering with us, kindly reply to this email, and we’ll arrange a quick call to discuss further steps on how we can collaborate effectively.

Thank you for considering this collaboration! Let’s make this festive season a celebration of learning, growth, and skill-building!

Warm regards,
Learning Utsav
